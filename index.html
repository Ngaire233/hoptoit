About Hop To It Organizing

I'm Ngaire, and I spent years managing complex software projects where everyone had different priorities and timelines. My specialty was filtering through the chaos, understanding what really mattered, and creating clear plans that delivered real value.

Sound familiar? It's exactly what happens in your home.

Your home has the same challenges as those software projects: different users, competing needs, limited resources. That's when I realized Agile methodology could work here too - breaking organizing into manageable sprints and a few iterations instead of overwhelming complete overhauls, focusing on solutions that fit your real life.

Services

Whether you need a complete home transformation or help with specific spaces, my Agile-inspired approach ensures personalized, efficient, and sustainable organizing solutions.

Full Home Organization

Using Agile methodology, we break your entire home into manageable projects. Each 'sprint' focuses on specific areas, adapts to what's working, and builds momentum toward a completely organized home that fits your lifestyle.

Room-by-Room Solutions

Perfect for targeting your biggest pain points first. We deep-dive into single spaces using focused sprints, gathering 'user stories' from everyone who uses that room to create systems that actually work for real life.

System Tune-ups & Bug Backlog

Your organized systems need regular maintenance, just like software. We address the 'technical debt' that builds up - those quick fixes and workarounds that gradually undermine your organized spaces.

My Agile-Inspired Process

Using principles from Agile project management, we break down organizing projects into manageable steps, making the process stress-free and effective.

1

Backlog Creation

We'll walk through your home and create a master list of all spaces to organize, breaking each into manageable tasks.

2

Sprint Planning

Together we'll choose which areas to focus on first and create a timeline that fits your schedule.

3

Sprint Execution

We'll tackle the agreed-on tasks step by step with hands-on organizing and implementation.

4

Review & Iterate

The magic happens as we work through your space, continuously refining systems based on what we discover to ensure your space truly supports how your family lives.
