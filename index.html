About Hop To It Organizing

Welcome to Hop To It Organizing! I'm a British/New Zealand professional organizer with a background in software product management and a passion for creating calm, functional spaces.

After years of helping teams deliver complex projects using Agile methodology, I realized these same principles could transform how we organize our homes. Breaking big projects into manageable tasks, adapting to your needs, and focusing on what matters most to you.

As an expat myself, I understand the unique challenges of setting up home in China, managing frequent relocations, and dealing with accumulation of items. My mission is to help you create beautiful, uncluttered spaces that work for your lifestyle.

Our Agile-Inspired Process

Using principles from Agile project management, we break down organizing projects into manageable steps, making the process stress-free and effective.

1

Backlog Creation

We'll walk through your home and create a master list of all spaces to organize, breaking each into manageable tasks.

2

Sprint Planning

Together we'll choose which areas to focus on first and create a timeline that fits your schedule.

3

Sprint Execution

We'll tackle the agreed-on tasks step by step with hands-on organizing and implementation.

4

Review & Adjust

We'll review progress, make any needed adjustments, and plan the next steps for continued success.
