About Hop To It Organizing

I'm an organizer who discovered my calling through an unexpected path - managing complex software projects that seemed impossible to untangle.

For years, I specialized in taking complex software development environments where feedback was coming from every direction - stakeholders, users, developers, executives - each with different priorities, timelines, and constraints. My job was to make sense of it all: filter through conflicting requirements, understand the real business needs, work within budget and time limitations, and create clear action plans that actually delivered value to end users.

Sound familiar? It's exactly what happens in your home.

Just like those software projects, your home has multiple "users" (family members, pets, guests), competing needs (storage vs. aesthetics, convenience vs. tidiness), and real-world constraints (space, budget, time). The difference is, while I used to organize code and features, now I organize closets and kitchens - and the principles are remarkably similar.

That's how I discovered that Agile methodology - the same approach that helped me deliver complex projects on time and within scope - could transform how we organize our homes. Instead of overwhelming "complete overhauls," we break everything into manageable tasks, adapt to what's working, and focus on what matters most to you and your family.

As a fellow expat in China, I understand the unique challenges of frequent moves and creating functional homes in rental spaces. My mission is to help you build organizing systems that work for your lifestyle and adapt with you through life's changes.

My Agile-Inspired Process

Using principles from Agile project management, we break down organizing projects into manageable steps, making the process stress-free and effective.

1

Backlog Creation

We'll walk through your home and create a master list of all spaces to organize, breaking each into manageable tasks.

2

Sprint Planning

Together we'll choose which areas to focus on first and create a timeline that fits your schedule.

3

Sprint Execution

We'll tackle the agreed-on tasks step by step with hands-on organizing and implementation.

4

Review & Adjust

We'll review progress, make any needed adjustments, and plan the next steps for continued success.
